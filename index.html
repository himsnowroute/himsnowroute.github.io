Exclusive Manali Tour

5 Nights / 6 Days Itinerary (Day 5 & 6)

High-Altitude Thrills at Solang Valley Day 5

Morning

Head up to Solang Valley, widely known as the adventure capital of Manali, right after a delicious breakfast at your hotel.

Afternoon

Soar high into the sky on an exhilarating tandem paragliding ride with your partner, or simply sit back and enjoy the panoramic snow-capped mountain views from the Solang Ropeway cable car.

Evening

Return to Manali. Spend a relaxed final evening shopping for local wooden crafts, authentic saffron, and fresh fruit wines on the vibrant Mall Road.

Optional Upgrade: If the weather permits and roads are open, you can upgrade your itinerary to visit the high-altitude Atal Tunnel and the stunning Sissu Valley.

Manali to Delhi Return Journey Day 6

Morning

Enjoy a hearty breakfast with a breathtaking view of the snow peaks. Check out from your hotel and stop at a Kullu Shawl Factory on the highway to buy authentic hand-woven pashminas, stoles, or traditional caps as lifelong memories.

Night (540 km / ~11-12 hours)

Rest easy as your professional driver smoothly navigates the downhill highways, dropping you back safely at your preferred location in Delhi late at night.
